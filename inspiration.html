Life brings us as many joyful moments as it does downfalls,
and although there are days we wish there was a manual to follow,
it simply wouldn't be the same without the spontaneity.


You fall, you rise, you make mistakes, you live, you learn.
You are human not perfect.


I know sometimes you feel like giving up.
I know you have good days and bad days.
I know everyday you question yourself.
I know you have more questions than answers.
You have to rise above the waters of your soul and bloom, no matter how hot the fire is.
No matter how many arrows you carry you carry on your back, believe me, you are a warrior.
You are a soldier filled with both pain and love.
And life, well life is just another beast you were meant to tame.


The journey of life may not become easier as we grow older,
but we do seem to understand it better as our perspectives evolve.


You have so much potential and so many things left to do.
You have time. Better things are coming your way.
Please hang in there. You can do it!
